About Our NGO

Who We Are

InAmigos Foundation is a social organization dedicated to creating positive impact in society through education, women empowerment, environmental awareness, animal welfare, and food distribution programs.

Our mission is to inspire communities and bring meaningful change through collective efforts and volunteer support.
